## 1. Project Overview

This quotation covers the design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance of a web-based Information System for Azani Internet Service Provider. The system will manage institution registrations, bandwidth subscriptions, payment processing, infrastructure tracking, service upgrades, late payment fines, disconnection/reconnection workflows, and comprehensive reporting.

The system will be developed using Node.js with Express.js, MySQL database, and a responsive web-based frontend, following the Waterfall SDLC methodology over a 7-month project timeline (January–July 2026).

### General Terms

1. **Deposit Requirement:** A 50% deposit of the system development cost (KSh 60,000) is required before development work commences. No work will begin without receipt of the deposit.

2. **Final Payment:** The remaining 50% (KSh 60,000) is due upon successful completion of User Acceptance Testing (UAT) and system handover to the client.

3. **Scope of Work:** This quotation covers the system as described in the Software Requirements Specification (SRS). Any additional features or changes beyond the agreed scope will be subject to a separate change request and additional charges.

4. **Intellectual Property:** Upon full payment, the client receives full ownership of the system source code and all associated documentation.

5. **Infrastructure Costs:** Infrastructure items (PCs, LAN nodes, installation) are optional and billed separately at the rates specified above.

6. **Maintenance Contract:** The maintenance contract is optional but recommended. It begins after the warranty period and is billed monthly or annually as agreed.

7. **Warranty Period:** A 30-day warranty period is included after system deployment, covering bug fixes at no additional cost.

8. **Support Hours:** Technical support is available Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Emergency support outside these hours is available with a 24-hour response time.

9. **Cancellation:** In the event of project cancellation by the client, the deposit is non-refundable. Work completed up to the point of cancellation will be delivered to the client.

10. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all project-related information, including business data, system credentials, and proprietary processes.
